# Brainstorming & Requirements Analysis

Guide structured brainstorming and requirements analysis sessions to help clarify goals, constraints, and solutions.

## When to Use

- User needs to explore ideas or solutions
- Requirements are vague or incomplete
- Multiple approaches need to be evaluated
- Creative problem-solving is needed

## Process

1. **Clarify Context**: Understand the domain, goals, and constraints
2. **Generate Ideas**: Use structured techniques (divergent thinking)
3. **Evaluate & Filter**: Apply criteria to narrow down (convergent thinking)
4. **Synthesize**: Combine best ideas into coherent approach
5. **Document**: Capture decisions, trade-offs, and rationale

## Techniques

- **SCAMPER**: Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other use, Eliminate, Reverse
- **Mind Mapping**: Visual exploration of connected concepts
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
- **First Principles**: Break down to fundamental truths and rebuild
- **Reverse Brainstorming**: How could we cause the opposite problem?

# Neat Freak: Session Knowledge Organization

Organizes and consolidates knowledge from completed work sessions.

## When to Use

At the end of a session or when:
- A significant feature was completed
- Important decisions were made
- New patterns or conventions were established
- Documentation needs updating

## Process

1. **Review Session Work**: What was accomplished
2. **Extract Decisions**: Capture key decisions and their rationale
3. **Document Patterns**: New conventions, workarounds, or patterns discovered
4. **Update Knowledge Base**: Update AGENTS.md, README, or relevant docs
5. **Capture Gotchas**: Anything that was tricky or non-obvious
6. **List Next Steps**: What remains to be done

## Output

Provide a structured summary:
- **Accomplished**: What was done
- **Decisions**: Key choices made and why
- **Patterns**: Things to remember for future
- **Tech Debt**: Things left imperfect
- **Next Steps**: Priority actions for next session
